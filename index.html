Our Heritage

A Family Legacy
Served with Love

Born from generations of North Indian culinary tradition, Bombay Indian Restaurant brings authentic flavours to the sunlit shores of Santa Ponsa. Our family recipes, passed down with care, meet the finest local ingredients of Mallorca.

Every dish honours our roots — aromatic spice blends ground fresh each morning, the tandoor fired to perfection, naan baked with devotion. This is more than a restaurant. It is our home, and you are always welcome at our table.
